Hi there! This website is to show people what I do and what I'm interested in. I strive to learn as much as I can and to use that knowledge to help others.


I started my computing journey during the COVID Lockdowns that took place in the UK throughout 2020/21. Prior to this I didn't know anything about computing and wasn't interested in it. I first started out learning about HTML and CSS before continuing into other niches such as Python, Java etc.


Upon the reintroduction of in-person school lessons, I blazed through the coursework provided by my teacher. He decided that I would be better off moving up classes to the Higher Class in 2022. Once completing my Higher Exam for computing, I continued my study through a college link course, further expanding my knowledge on computing paradigms and more complex programming techniques. As of 2023, I am holding a conditional offer for the BSc Software Engineering Course with the University of Strathclyde, hoping to further expand my knowledge into even more advanced and complex topics.
